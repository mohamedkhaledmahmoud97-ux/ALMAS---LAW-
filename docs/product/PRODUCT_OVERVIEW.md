# Product Overview

ALMAS LAW is being structured as an enterprise-grade AI platform for Arabic legal intelligence, trusted reasoning, and secure collaboration.

## Product Goals

- Provide trustworthy legal AI workflows for enterprise teams.
- Support explainable multi-agent reasoning.
- Enable secure deployment for regulated and sensitive use cases.

## Delivery Principles

- Security by design
- Transparency and auditability
- Modular architecture
- Scalable collaboration
